Keep It Neat

Software that respects your attention.

Software has gotten worse. Ads, trackers, accounts, notifications, dark patterns, upsells — designed to extract your time and attention.

Keep It Neat is a small series of replacement apps for things you do every day. The goal isn't to be the biggest or the best. Just to do the actual thing, without the rest.

The promise

N No tracking.
No analytics, no telemetry, no third-party scripts. When data has to be stored, it's the minimum and serves you — never sold, never shared, never used for ads.
E Easy.
No account required where it's avoidable. Loads fast. Works offline.
A Ad-free.
No ads. Period.
T Thoughtful.
No notifications you didn't ask for. No dark patterns. No subscriptions or upsell tiers — free, or one clear price.
