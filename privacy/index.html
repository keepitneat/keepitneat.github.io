How we use your data

We don't.

Keep It Neat doesn't track you. No analytics, no telemetry, no cookies, no third-party scripts. This site doesn't even pull fonts from someone else's server — they're bundled right here.

Each app keeps your data on your device. A couple need to call an outside service to do their actual job — Just the Weather sends your location to the National Weather Service to fetch a forecast — and every app spells out its own exceptions on its own privacy page.

This site is open source and hosted on GitHub Pages — read every line.
